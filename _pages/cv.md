Research Experience
======
* **2022 Spring: Probing Absorption Spectrum of Dark Exciton of Monolayer WS2**
  * **Institute:** School of Physics and Technology, Wuhan University
  * **Advisor:** Prof. Shunping Zhang **Co-worker:** Yuze Lu, Xiaohe Shang
  * Researched the absorption spectrum of dark exciton of monolayer WS2 directly by radially polarized beams.
  * Prepared 2-D WS2 material by mechanical exfoliation method.
  * Used the modulation of phase information on the Liquid Crystal of Silicon (LCOS) of Spatial Light Modulator (SLM) and of the beam’s polarization state through two quarter‑wave plates to prepare the radially polarized beams experimentally.
  * Programmed with Labview to control the super continuous laser source, SLM and the spectrometer in which way simultaneously execute the conversion of wavelength, the modulation of the spatial phase of light and spectral acquisition, to eliminate the influence of low frequency background noise that vibrated over time.
